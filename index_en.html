The German-Turkish Automotive Summit brings together leading representatives from industry, science and politics to advance bilateral cooperation in the automotive sector – at a time when the industry faces profound transformation processes.

Germany and Türkiye have been closely connected for decades: as manufacturing locations, as trading partners – and as drivers of technological innovation. While Türkiye has developed into one of the most important production and export hubs for German automotive manufacturers and suppliers, German companies set impulses for the future of mobility with their technological know-how.

The summit offers a high-profile platform for dialogue on current challenges and future opportunities – from electromobility and digitalisation to sustainable production strategies. The focus is on investment potential, resilient supply chains and strategic cooperation. The exchange of knowledge between leading decision-makers is intended to foster synergies, connect markets and enable sustainable value creation in both countries.
